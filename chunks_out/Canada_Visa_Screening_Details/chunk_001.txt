Canada Visa Screening Requirements - Detailed
Overview
This document summarises visa screening requirements for Canada (Temporary Resident Visas,
Study Permits, Work Permits, and Permanent Residence applications), using official IRCC
guidance.
Canada places emphasis on admissibility (including criminality and health), proof of funds, and
purpose of travel.
Primary Identity & Application Forms
Valid passport, completed application forms specific to visa type (e.g., study permit application
packages include IMM forms).
Proof of payment for application fees and biometrics fee where applicable.
Health & Medical Examination
Permanent residents and some temporary residents must undergo a medical exam performed by
an IRCC-approved panel physician. Requirements vary by visa type.
Medical exams are designed to assess public health risk and may be requested during application
processing.
