Police Certificates & Background Screening
Applicants are often required to provide police certificates from countries where they have lived for
a certain period. Certificates must be translated into English or French if necessary.
Background checks assess criminal admissibility and prior immigration history.
Biometrics & VACs
Biometric data (fingerprints and photo) collection is required for most visa applicants and is
performed at Visa Application Centers (VACs).
Proof of Funds & Purpose
Students: proof of tuition payment or Guaranteed Investment Certificate (GIC) where applicable,
and letter of acceptance.
Workers: employment offer letters, LMIA or employer compliance documents where needed, and
evidence of salary/payment.
