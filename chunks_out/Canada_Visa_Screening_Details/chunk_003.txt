Common Reasons for Refusal & Tips
Insufficient funds, missing police certificates or medical exams, inconsistent documentation, and not
meeting the specific eligibility criteria are common refusal reasons.
Follow IRCC checklists closely and provide certified translations for non-English/French
documents.
Practical Checklist (What to Include)
Passport, completed application forms, photos, police certificates, medical exam (when required),
proof of funds, acceptance letters or job offers, biometrics receipt.
