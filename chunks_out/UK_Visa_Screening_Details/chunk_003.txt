Biometrics & Interview
Biometric enrolment (fingerprints and photo) at a Visa Application Centre (VAC) is required for
many non-EEA applicants. Some applicants may be called for an interview or further administrative

checks.
Common Reasons for Refusal & Tips
Insufficient proof of funds, weak ties to home country, discrepancies in the application, or missing
documents are common causes of refusal.
Prepare clear, dated, and translated documents where necessary; use certified translations for
non-English evidence.
Practical Checklist (What to Include)
Passport copy, application form, payment receipt, recent photos, bank statements,
employment/study proof, accommodation proof, travel itinerary, invitation letters, and any police
certificates if requested.
