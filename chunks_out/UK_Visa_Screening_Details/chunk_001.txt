UK Visa Screening Requirements - Detailed
Overview
This document summarises the key visa screening and application requirements for the United
Kingdom (UK). It is intended for project use and is compiled from official UK government guidance.
The most relevant UK documents include the Visit visa supporting documents guidance and the
Immigration System Statistics which explain what officers check when assessing visa applications.
Primary Identity & Travel Documents
Valid passport or travel document with at least one blank page. Passport should be valid for travel;
some applications recommend 6 months' validity.
Recent passport-sized photographs according to UK specifications. Certified copies of previous
passports if available.
Proof of Purpose & Supporting Evidence
Visitors: travel itinerary, accommodation bookings, letter of invitation from host, proof of return or
onward travel.
Students: Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) from the sponsor institution, proof of
course detail
