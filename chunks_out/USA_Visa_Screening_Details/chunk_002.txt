Biometrics, VAC & Interview
Biometric capture (fingerprints and photograph) is usually taken at a Visa Application Center (VAC)
before the consular interview.
An in-person interview at the consulate is often required; officers assess ties to the home country,
purpose of travel, and eligibility.
Health & Other Checks
Medical examinations are required for many immigrant visa categories and some long-term stays;
vaccinations may be required depending on the visa type.
Common Reasons for Refusal & Tips
Lack of strong home ties, insufficient documentation, misrepresentation or fraud, and unresolved
security or criminal history can lead to refusal.
Be honest during the interview and bring original documents; prepare concise answers that match
submitted paperwork.
