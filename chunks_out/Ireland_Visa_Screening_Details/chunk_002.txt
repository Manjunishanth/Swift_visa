Biometrics, Police Certificates & Health Checks
Biometrics may be required for applicants outside specific exemptions; police certificates may be
requested for long-term stays or certain nationalities.
Medical or TB checks may be requested depending on applicant origin and intended length of stay.
Processing, Refusal Reasons & Tips
Refusals commonly result from insufficient funds, incomplete documentation, unclear purpose of
travel, or previous immigration history issues.
Be thorough with translations, provide certified copies where needed, and follow the INIS required
document lists for each visa type.
Practical Checklist (What to Include)
Passport, application form, photos, fee payment receipt, proof of funds, accommodation proof,
invitation or offer letters, police certificates (if requested), and biometric enrolment where required.
