Ireland Visa Screening Requirements - Detailed
Overview
This document summarises visa screening requirements for the Republic of Ireland, based on INIS
guidance and application FAQs.
Ireland operates its own visa policy and application system (AVATS/INIS) and requires supporting
documents similar to other EU and OECD countries.
Primary Identity & Application Forms
Valid passport and completed AVATS application or online form; passport should generally have at
least 6 months validity remaining for travel.
Two recent passport sized photographs and copies of previous passports where applicable.
Purpose, Funds & Accommodation
Proof of purpose: invitation letters, proof of enrolment for students, job offer letters for workers or
itinerary for visitors.
Proof of sufficient funds and accommodation during the stay; bank statements, host letters, or hotel
bookings may be accepted.
